Adam Strange

I'm a Los Angeles based painter and Illustrator. I've done editorial illustrations for several prominent publications including the New York Times. My paintings have been featured in print publications in the US and abroad. I've shown work at the La Luz De Jesus Gallery in Hollywood California since 2006 including a sold out exhibition (12 paintings) in 2007. In 2011 my work was featured along with the most influential artists in pop surrealism (Robert Williams, Matt Groening, Gary Baseman, The Clayton Brothers, Glenn Barr, Tara McPherson) in the book "La Luz de Jesus 25: The Little Gallery That Could." This book is an anthology of all the artists who've had solo exhibitions at this important and influential gallery in the last 25 years. Through the gallery I've sold work to many outstanding collectors and celebrities including Danny Elfman, Patricia Arquette and Rickey Lee Jones.
